SCORM → PDF Transformer

Upload a SCORM package to extract its content and convert it into a readable PDF document. The tool works in two modes: basic extraction produces a structured PDF from the raw slide content, and prose rewrite uses Claude to rewrite that content as flowing, readable paragraphs — similar to a reading guide or course handbook. The rewrite feature requires your own Anthropic API key.
